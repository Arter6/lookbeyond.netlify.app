Space

What do we know about it?

NOTHING

Indeed, we barely know anything about it. All our knowledge about it is speculation that is based on theories and observations. And observations is what this website is dedicated to. Until the middle of previous century, people watched the sky only from the ground, but after the launch of the first spacecraft, it became clear that a new stage in space exploration had come. First "Sputnik-1", then "Explorer-1", "Ariel 1"... And in 1990, the legendary Hubble telescope was launched. Thanks to it, scientists have managed to make many discoveries, even those for the study of which the telescope was not originally intended. But it wasn't the only spacecraft used by scientists, there were and will be many others. You can read more about them on this website.
